The opportunity, in plain terms

The Lateral Entry Entrance Test (LEET) is how a diploma holder in Jammu & Kashmir upgrades a diploma into a degree — joining a B.Tech / B.E. degree directly in the second year instead of starting over in the first. You skip the first year, so you graduate with a full B.Tech in three more years instead of four — the same degree, less time and cost.

What it unlocks for you

  • Better roles & pay: a degree opens jobs and pay bands a diploma alone often can't.
  • Government & PSU paths: become eligible for GATE, PSU recruitment and degree-level engineering posts.
  • Campus placements: sit for the same recruiters as any B.Tech student in Jammu & Kashmir.
  • Higher studies: M.Tech, MBA and beyond become options.

Frequently asked questions

What are the options after a diploma in Jammu & Kashmir?

Take a job on your diploma, upgrade to B.Tech through lateral entry in Jammu & Kashmir, or restart a degree via JEE. Lateral entry is the quickest route to a full engineering degree because it credits your first year.

Is lateral entry worth it in Jammu & Kashmir?
